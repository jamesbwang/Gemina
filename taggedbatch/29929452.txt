 Violence against women ( VAW) is a major public health problem globally , particularly in developing countries including Ethiopia. Accordingly , sustainable development goal ( SDG) 5 ( by United Nation) prioritizes VAW and calls for the design and implementation of programs relevant to its elimination by 2030. However , little is known about the epidemiology of VAW as all estimates from few , cross-sectional studies so far are inconsistent and inconclusive. This study , therefore , was conducted to determine the pooled national prevalence of VAW in Ethiopia. Databases including PUBMED , MEDLINE , Cochrane review , CINAHL , African Journals Online , and Google Scholar were reviewed , using relevant search engines.
