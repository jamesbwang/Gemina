 European Consensus Platform for Alternatives ( ecopa) and Scandinavian Society for Cell Toxicology ( SSCT) hosted the joint ecopa-SSCT workshop `` Up-to-date in vitro approaches in regulatory risk assessment and disease modeling '' in Helsinki , 14-16 June 2017. The workshop consisted of three scientific sessions and poster sessions , all together 28 oral and 17 poster presentations. Further , a debate session on ` Human cellular or animal disease models: pros and cons to mimic human effects ' was organized with Ian Cotgreave ( Swetox , Sweden) and Matti Poutanen ( University of Turku , Finland) as primary debaters to introduce opposing statements.
