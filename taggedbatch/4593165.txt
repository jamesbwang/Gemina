 Urethral catheterization is a common invasive procedure applied for several purposes on children. Voiding cystourethrogram ( VCUG) is a diagnostic fluoroscopic procedure , which is commonly used in assessing urinary<disease> tract<disease> infections<disease> in children and which requires urethral catheterization. We report a case of intravesical catheter knotting , which is a rare complication of VCUG.
