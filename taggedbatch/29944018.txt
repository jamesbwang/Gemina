 Interleukin-9 ( IL-9) is a multifunctional cytokine involved in protective immunity or immunopathology depending on the microenvironment and specific disease settings. Our early study determined that IL-9 and Th9 cells participate in and promote the progression of experimental autoimmune myasthenia gravis ( EAMG). The data from this study showed that exogenous recombinant rat IL-9 ( rrIL-9) acted as an IL-9 receptor antagonist , reduced the incidence of EAMG in rats , alleviated the severity of the disease , and reduced the anti-acetylcholine receptor ( AChR) IgG antibody levels by altering the Th-subset distribution.
