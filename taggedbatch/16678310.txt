 Polydextrose ( PDX) ingestion may increase the intestinal absorption of iron. This study evaluated the effects of 7.5 % polydextrose supplementation on markers of iron uptake , transport and storage in partially gastrectomized rats. Half of a batch of 40 male Wistar rats ( 250 g) underwent Billroth II partial gastrectomy with anterior truncal vagotomy ( GXT) , while the other half underwent sham gastrectomy ( SHAM).
