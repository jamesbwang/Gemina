 Hepatic fibrosis<disease> is a common feature of almost all chronic liver diseases. Formation of new vessels ( angiogenesis) is a process strictly related to the progressive fibrogenesis which leads to cirrhosis and liver cancer. This review mainly concerns the relationship between angiogenesis and hepatic fibrosis<disease> , by considering the mechanism of angiogenesis , cells in angiogenesis , anti-angiogenic and Chinese medicine therapies.
