 Most working women do not meet current guidelines for physical activity ( PA). A 12-week pilot study tested a workplace , peer modeling PA intervention. Inactive female employees from a health system were randomized to an attention control group ( ACG; n = 26) or an intervention group ( IG; n = 26). The ACG received health information.
