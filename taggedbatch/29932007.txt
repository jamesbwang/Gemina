 There is limited understanding about the obesity-related behaviors of diet , physical activity , and sedentary behavior in Korean American women. The purpose of this study was to cluster obesity-related behavior patterns of Korean American women and to examine group differences in acculturation , socioeconomic status ( SES) , and body mass index ( BMI).
