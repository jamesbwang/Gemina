 The Dysregulation Profile ( DP) has emerged as a measure of concurrent affective , behavioral and cognitive dysregulation , associated with severe psychopathology , and poor adjustment. While originally developed with the Child Behavior Checklist , more recently the DP has also been defined on the Strengths and Difficulties Questionnaire ( SDQ) , mostly with a 5-item , but also a 15-item , SDQ-DP measure. This study evaluated the SDQ-DP by examining its factor structure , measurement invariance , and construct validity. Different SDQ-DP operationalizations were compared.
