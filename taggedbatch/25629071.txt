 This study was carried out to determine the infestation of domestic ruminants to ticks in an important livestock-rearing region , located in central part of Iran. Ticks were collected from cattle , sheep , and goats and then were identified with appropriate identification keys to species level in two different ecological regions of plains and mountain<disease> in 4 seasons in 2015. Totally 492 ticks from cattle , sheep , and goats in 34 herds were collected. Totally , 18.53 % of domestic animals were infected by ticks. Annually , many livestock products are exported to other parts from this region; therefore , it is very important to identify the infection rate of tick-borne diseases as well as safety factors on livestock.
