 A small library of novel quinoline-3-carbaldehyde hydrazones ( Series 1) , acylhydrazones ( Series 2) , and arylsulfonylhydrazones ( Series 3) bearing either a 1,2,4-triazole or benzotriazole ring at position 2 was prepared , characterized by elemental analyses and IR , NMR , and MS spectra , and then subjected to in vitro cytotoxicity studies on three human tumor cell lines: DAN-G , LCLC-103H , and SISO.
