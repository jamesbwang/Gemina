 The certainty that from the pharmacological abuse , resulting in a high-frequency or chronic migraine , the patient can be redeemed is now a well-defined assumption , even if the ways of rehabilitation are different and motivated by the context where they originate ( 1). We have different rehabilitation proposals , infusional/oral , progressive/immediate , with/without psychological support in a protected environment/at home.
