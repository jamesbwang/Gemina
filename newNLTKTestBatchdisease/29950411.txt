 Herpesvirus<GPE> infections are highly prevalent in the human population and persist for lifetime. They are often acquired subclinically but potentially progress to life-threatening diseases in immunocompromised individuals. The interferon system is indispensable for the control of herpesviral replication. However, the responsible antiviral effector mechanisms are not well characterized.
