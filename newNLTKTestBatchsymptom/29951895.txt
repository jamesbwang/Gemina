 This article reports the findings from a study that examined the role of economic stress and coping resources in predicting hwabyung symptoms among Koreans in the United States<GPE>. The literal meaning of hwabyung is `` fire illness '' or `` anger illness. '' Koreans believe that chronic stress can cause the onset of hwabyung, manifested mainly through somatic symptoms.
