  Osteoarthritis is a joint disease characterized by cartilage degradation , altered gene expression and inflammation<symptom>. NOTCH1 and NOTCH2 receptors and the JAGGED1 ligand regulate chondrocyte biology; however , the contribution of Notch signaling to osteoarthritis is controversial. Hajdu Cheney Syndrome<disease> ( HCS) is a rare genetic disorder affecting the skeleton and associated with NOTCH2 mutations that lead to NOTCH2 gain-of-function.
