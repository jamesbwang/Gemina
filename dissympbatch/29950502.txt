  Brown-Vialetto-Van Laere syndrome<disease> ( BVVLS) or riboflavin transporter deficiency ( OMIM 211530) is a rare treatable autosomal recessive neurodegenerative disorder. This condition is associated with progressive pontobulbar palsy. We describe the clinical course of a 16-month-old boy with BVVLS and a novel homozygous mutation from Pakistan. Our patient presented with stridor and respiratory insufficiency. Hearing loss which is the most common sign of this condition was absent , making it an unusual presentation of BVVLS.
