  Sinus of valsalva aneurysm<disease> ( SVA) is a rare cardiac disease. The most common complication of SVA is rupture into the right atrium or right ventricle. Rupture into the left chambers is very rare. Transthoracic echocardiography and transesophageal echocardiography showed rupture of a noncoronary SVA into the left atrium.
