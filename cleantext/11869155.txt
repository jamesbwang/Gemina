  Falls are common among older adults. However , not much is known about the prevalence of falls among older patients with cancer. In 2015 , older patients with cancer referred to Geriatrics service for preoperative evaluation were assessed for fall history , basic and instrumental activities of daily living ( ADL and IADL) , KPS , and use of assistive device. Of 806 patients , 215 ( 26.7 %) patients reported fall. Incidence of last fall inside and outside home was 54.4 % and 45.5 % , respectively.
