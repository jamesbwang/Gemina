  The Attention Training Technique ( ATT) and Mindful Self-Compassion ( MSC) are two promising psychological interventions. ATT is a 12-min auditory exercise designed to strengthen attentional control and promote external focus of attention , while MSC uses guided meditation and exercises designed to promote self-compassion.
