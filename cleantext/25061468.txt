  High sodium intake is a major public health concern. Sodium consumption pattern of West<disease> Indian population has never been reported before. The cross-sectional study assessed sodium intake pattern by considering all possible dietary sources and spot urine sodium estimation among sedentary healthy adults of productive age group ( 35-55 years).
