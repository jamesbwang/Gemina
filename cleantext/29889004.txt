  At the end of the summer of 2016 , unusually high levels of mortality were detected in Passeriformes and Strigiformes in Flanders , Belgium , mainly in European Blackbirds ( Turdus merula). A passive surveillance program demonstrated a widespread Usutu<pathogen> virus<pathogen> outbreak and revealed a coinfection with Plasmodium<pathogen> in 99 % of the dead passerine birds that were necropsied.
