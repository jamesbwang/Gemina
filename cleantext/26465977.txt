  Food hygiene is a key factor at the time of production and distribution of food. Therefore , the present study aimed to assess the interventional role of education in changing the knowledge and attitudes of urbane mothers towards food hygiene in Ravansar Township , Kermanshah , Iran. To this end , 200 mothers residing in Ravansar Township were selected using simple random sampling. First , the subjects ' knowledge and attitudes towards food hygiene were evaluated in a pre-test , and then after holding some educational sessions , the two variables were assessed again in a post-test using a researcher-made questionnaire with 72 questions. The reliability and validity of the questionnaire were evaluated using Cronbach 's alpha and content validity , respectively.
