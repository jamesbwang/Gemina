  Adjuvant endocrine therapy ( AET) taken for a minimum of five years reduces the recurrence and mortality risks among women with hormone-sensitive breast cancer. However , adherence to AET is suboptimal. To guide the development of theory-based interventions to enhance AET adherence , we conducted a study to explore beliefs regarding early adherence to AET.
