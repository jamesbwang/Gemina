  Envenomation by Bothrops snakes promotes the release of inflammatory mediators , whose effects during this context are not well understood. These mediators include chemokines , cytokines and eicosanoids. Indeed , Bothrops snake envenomation results in local and systemic perturbations , including leukocyte recruitment , edema<symptom> , pain and extensive tissue damage.
