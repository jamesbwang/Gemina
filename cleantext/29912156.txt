  Arterial hypertension is one of the main risk factors in the development of cardiovascular diseases. Therefore , it is important to look for new drugs to treat hypertension. In this study , we carried out the screening of 19 compounds ( triterpenes , diterpenes , sesquiterpenes , lignans , and flavonoids) isolated from 10 plants used in Mexican traditional medicine to determine whether they elicited vascular smooth muscle relaxation and , therefore , could represent novel anti-hypertension drug candidates.
