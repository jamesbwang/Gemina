  The aim of this study was to investigate how the Public Dental Service ( PDS) in Sweden has managed to maintain a market position at a time of change in political ideologies and increased competition from a growing private sector. All Chief Dental Officers ( CDOs) , who had held this leading position for at least 5 years ( All the CDOs answered that they had influenced outcomes through brand building and core value work , related to both patients and employees , and to a lesser extent through competitor analysis. Some CDOs had a slightly different approach to the way they described visions<symptom> , strategies and short-term goals. They used more business-oriented concepts , such as customers , market shares , and profits. Most CDOs regarded their actions as important for the successful development of their organization. The PDS appears to have a stable , strong position in the Swedish dental care market , and a great deal of effort has been put into consolidating this position.
