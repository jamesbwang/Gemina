  Behçet 's disease is a multisystem inflammatory disorder. Cardiovascular involvement is rare but may present with various manifestations , resulting in high mortality. In this article , we report a young male who had chest<symptom> pain<symptom> accompanied by systemic involvement , and was diagnosed of Behçet 's disease with serious pulmonary artery and coronary thrombosis by systemic check-up. Finally , the patient was successfully treated with a combination of prednisone and immunosuppressive agents instead of surgery. To our knowledge , this is the first report of concurrent coronary thrombosis and pulmonary artery thrombosis in Behçet 's disease patients.
