  The Association of Southeast Asian Nations ( ASEAN) is a diverse region that is experiencing economic growth and increased non-communicable disease burden. This paper aims to evaluate the current regulations , dietary recommendations and research related to whole grains in this region. To do this , a systematic literature review was carried out and information was collected on regulations and dietary recommendations from each member state.
