  Dietary consumption of contaminated vegetables may contribute to polycyclic aromatic hydrocarbon ( PAH) exposure in humans; however , this exposure pathway has not been examined thoroughly. This study aims to characterize the concentrations of PAHs in six types of vegetables grown near industrial facilities in Shanghai , China. We analyzed 16 individual PAHs on the US EPA priority list , and the total concentration in vegetables ranged from 65.7 to 458.0 ng g
