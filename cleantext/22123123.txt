  To ascertain and analyze the indications for transfusion of blood components in obstetric practice at our center. A prospective observational study was conducted to analyze the various indications for transfusion of blood components in a tertiary care hospital. 1.3 % of all obstetric patients from our center had blood components transfusion during the study period. Postpartum hemorrhage<symptom> , placental causes and anemia<symptom> are the commonest causes for need of transfusion in obstetric practice.
