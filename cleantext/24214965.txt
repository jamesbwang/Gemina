  The World Wide Web is an indispensable tool for biomedical researchers who are striving to understand the molecular basis of phenotype. However , it presents challenges in the form of proliferation of data resources , with heterogeneity ranging from their content to functionality to interfaces. This often frustrates researchers who must visit multiple sites , become familiar with their interfaces , and learn how to use them to extract knowledge.
