  Langerhans cell histiocytosis ( LCH) of the orbit is a rare clinical entity with a diagnostic and therapeutic dilemma. This was a retrospective study of eight patients with orbital LCH. All eight patients in our series were male , and the mean age at presentation was 8 years ( median 6 years; range , 7 months-23 years).
