  A cross-sectional study was conducted to determine sero-prevalence of Peste des Petits Ruminants ( PPR) among goat population of outbreak suspected areas; Kaski and Syangja districts of Nepal. A total of 460 goat serum samples were tested by using c-ELISA for the presence of antibodies against PPR.
