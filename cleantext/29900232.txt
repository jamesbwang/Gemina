  This dataset includes data derived from camera trap surveys and questionnaire surveys relating to small carnivores in agro-ecosystems in the Vhembe Biosphere Reserve , South Africa. The data were collected as part of the study `` Predation by small mammalian carnivores in rural agro-ecosystems: An undervalued ecosystem service? '' ( Williams et al. , 2017a) ( 1). Camera trap locations were stratified by land use: settlement , crops , and grazing areas.
