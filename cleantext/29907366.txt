  Ribavirin is a molecule with antiviral activity against different viruses. In clinical practice , it has made its niche almost exclusively for the treatment of the hepatitisC virus. However , there are other diseases in which it could be of benefit and it has the advantage of being suitable for oral , intravenous and inhaled administration.
