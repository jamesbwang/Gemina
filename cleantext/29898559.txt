  Flash floods and debris flows are iconic hazards in mountainous regions with steep relief , high rainfall intensities , rapid snowmelt events , and abundant sediments. The cuesta landscapes of southern Germany hardly come to mind when dealing with such hazards. A series of heavy rainstorms dumping up to 140 mm in 2 h caused destructive flash floods and debris flows in May 2016.
