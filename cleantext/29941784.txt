  This study is aimed to estimate the levels of mercury emission related to amalgam fillings at dental institutions in order to provide basic data for establishing a management protocol for extracted mercury-containing amalgam fillings. We conducted a cross-sectional study at a dental clinic of a general hospital ( City of Daegu) , a dental hospital ( City of Daegu) , and five private dental clinics ( one in the South and four in the North Gyeongsang Province).
