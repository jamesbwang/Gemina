  The anomalous origin of coronaries is rare. The purpose of this case report is to show a rare anomalous origin of coronaries. A 64-year-old female presented with chest<symptom> pain<symptom> that was typical in nature. The patient had left heart catheterization that showed an anomalous origin of coronaries , where all the coronaries were arising from the right coronary cusp. The patient had a significant disease in the distal left anterior descending artery , but it was a small vessel. Medical management was chosen. This is a rare presentation of the coronary anatomy.
