  Chronic obstructive pulmonary disease ( COPD) is a growing cause of morbidity and mortality worldwide. Cigarette smoke ( CS) exposure , a major cause of COPD , dysregulates airway epithelial ion transport and diminishes airway surface liquid ( ASL) volume.
