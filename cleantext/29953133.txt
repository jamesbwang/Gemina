  The Shetland Isles are a place of breathtaking landscapes and pristine wildlife. As harsh as the weather can be , so warm and welcoming are the people and their proud half-Scottish , half-Scandinavian culture. Practising psychiatry in this northernmost outpost of the UK involves significant challenges. The authors were the only two psychiatrists based on this remote island<disease> group , which is home to 23 000 people.
