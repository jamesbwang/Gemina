  Adipose tissue is an important endocrine organ that secretes a number of adipokines , like Leptin ( LEP). The aim this study was to investigate the prevalence of single nucleotide polymorphisms in LEP gene ( LEP 3 ` UTR A/C , -2548 G/A) and LEPR ( K109R and Q223R) and their association with Leptin level and obesity. We recruited 169 non-obese ( body mass index ( BMI) = 24.51-3.69 kg/m
