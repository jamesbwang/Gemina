  The retina shares embryological derivation with the brain and may provide a new measurement of overall growth status , especially useful in resource-limited settings. Optical coherence tomography ( OCT) provides detailed quantification of retinal structures. We enrolled community-dwelling children ages 3-11 years old in Siaya , Kenya and Thimphu , Bhutan in 2016.
