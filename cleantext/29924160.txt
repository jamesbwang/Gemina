  To verify the effectiveness of nursing interventions based on the Imogene King 's Theory of Goal Attainment , on improving care for people with diabetes and adherence to treatment. Quasi-experimental , longitudinal , randomized , simple study in a Primary Health Care Unit , in the city of Fortaleza , Cear√° state , Brazil. The sample consisted of 60 people with diabetes , divided into intervention and control groups , whose collection occurred from February to August 2013. In the intervention group , a significant adherence of the patients to the goals defined in the study was found. In the control , there was improvement in some aspects of the treatment. With these results , it was possible to conclude the feasibility of using Theory of Goal Attainment in the positive aspects for adherence to diabetes treatment and improvement of quality of life.
