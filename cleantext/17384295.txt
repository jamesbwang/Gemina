  This study describes the first confirmed report of contagious ecthyma in Black Bengal goats from Tripura state , a North-Eastern state of India situated at the Indo-Bangladesh border. Outbreaks were characterized by the high rates of morbidity ( 58-67 %) , low mortality ( 8-10 %) and case fatality ( 11-15 %).
