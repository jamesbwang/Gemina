  Neurodegenerative diseases are a heterogeneous group of disorders that are characterized by the gradual loss of neurons. The development of effective neuroprotective agents to prevent and control neurodegenerative diseases is specifically important. Recently , there has been an increasing interest in selecting flavonoid compounds as potential neuroprotective agents , owing to their high effectiveness with low side effects.
