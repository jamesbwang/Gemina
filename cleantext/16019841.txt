  Simultaneous determination of twenty-seven mycotoxins in ready-to-eat food samples using & ldquo; Quick Easy Cheap Rough and Safe & rdquo; ( QuEChERS) extraction and chromatographic methods coupled to mass spectrometry in tandem is described in this study. Mycotoxins included in this survey were aflatoxins ( B ₁ , B ₂ , G ₁ , G ₂) , enniatins ( A , A ₁ , B , B ₁) , beauvericin ( BEA) , fumonisins ( FB ₁ , FB ₂) , sterigmatocystin ( STG) , deoxynivalenol ( DON) , 3-acetyl-deoxynivalenol ( 3-ADON) , 15-acetyl-deoxynivalenol ( 15-ADON) , nivalenol ( NIV) , neosolaniol ( NEO) , diacetoxyscirpenol ( DAS) , fusarenon-X ( FUS-X) , zearalenone ( ZEA) , & alpha;- zearalanol ( & alpha; ZAL) , & beta;- zearalenone ( & beta; ZAL) , & alpha;- zearalenol ( & alpha; ZOL) , & beta;- zearalenol ( & beta; zol) , T2 , and HT-2 toxin.
