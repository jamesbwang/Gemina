  The present prospective clinical observational study aimed to evaluate the efficacy of Toric intraocular lenses ( IOL) to achieve rotational stability and astigmatism correction in eyes with high myopia. A total of 27 consecutive cataract patients ( 39 eyes) with pre-existing corneal astigmatism ( 1.5-3.5 D) were divided into two groups according to their refractive status: One group of 18 eyes with high myopia- ( 12.5-6.0 D) and another group consisting of 21 eyes with emmetropia or low myopia ( -3.0-0.0 D).
