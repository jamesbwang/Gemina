  The synthesis of the structure , 1 , assigned to the anti-inflammatory natural product myrsinoic acid F is reported together with a means for preparing its Z-isomer 21. While neither of these compounds corresponds to the natural product , both of them are anti-inflammatory agents ( as determined using a mouse ear edema<symptom> assay) with congener 1 being notably more potent than the widely prescribed NSAID indometacin.
