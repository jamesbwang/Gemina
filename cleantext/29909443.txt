  Extreme flooding in May , 2014 affected the sub-catchments of six major rivers in Serbia. The goal of the study was to evaluate the contents of potentially toxic elements ( PTEs) As , Cd , Pb , Cr , Ni , Cu , and Zn in flood sediments and arable soils within the affected sub-catchments using regulatory guidelines and background levels.
