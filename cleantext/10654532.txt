  A case of the right pulmonary artery-to- left atrial fistula<disease> with atrial septal defect ( ASD) is presented. The fistula<disease> was detected after the patient developed desaturation following surgical closure of the ASD. It was managed with a transcatheter ( trans-RPA route) closure of the fistula<disease> using a 12-mm Amplatzer ventricular septal defect closure device.
