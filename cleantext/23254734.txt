  Multiple cutaneous and uterine leiomyomatosis ( MCUL) , also known as Reed 's syndrome<disease> , is a rare genodermatosis , with an autosomal dominant pattern of inheritance. It results from a germline heterozygous mutation of fumarate hydratase gene , that is classified as a tumor suppressor gene. Hereditary leiomyomatosis and renal cell cancer is characterized by the association of MCUL with renal cell carcinoma.
