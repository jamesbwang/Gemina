  Potentially malignant oral mucosal disease has some ability to give rise to malignancy of the oral epithelium , that is , oral squamous cell carcinoma ( OSCC). The present article provides a succinct review of the possible or probable causes of potentially premalignant oral epithelial lesions<symptom>. There is a focus upon studies that examined the causes or etiologic associations with clinically likely or histopathologically detectable oral epithelial dysplasia.
