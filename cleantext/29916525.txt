  Family caregivers of individuals with Alzheimer 's disease and related dementias ( ADRD) experience long-term mental health effects. Although caregivers who place relatives in long-term care ( LTC) experience increased depression<symptom> , anxiety<symptom> , and chronic grief post-placement , interventions to improve caregivers ' mental health have focused mainly on in-home care. Current researchers previously tested a group-based Chronic Grief Management Intervention ( CGMI) with ADRD caregivers of individuals in LTC , with significant effects on caregiver mental health outcomes.
