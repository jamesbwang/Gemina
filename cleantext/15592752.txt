  Eilenodontines are one of the oldest radiation of herbivorous lepidosaurs ( snakes , lizards and tuatara) characterized by batteries of wide teeth with thick enamel that bear mammal-like wear facets. Unlike most reptiles , eilenodontines have limited tooth replacement , making dental longevity particularly important to them.
