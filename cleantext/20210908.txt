  This study compares the effects of team-sport training , for sedentary men with lifestyle diseases , with fitness training in a pragmatic set-up in a community health centre ( CHC). Thirty-two men in the fitness group ( FiG) and 36 men in the team-sport group ( TsG) completed the training and trained for 60-90 min , two times/week for 12-16 weeks.
