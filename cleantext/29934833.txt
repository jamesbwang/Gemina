  Tunis 's Lake North ( LNT) , located on the Mediterranean Sea , and Tunisia 's most important lagoon due to its economic value and its strategic position within the city , has recently undergone a vast sanitation project. To study the lagoon 's level of metal pollution , three sediment cores were taken and the sediments were analyzed for trace and major elements , acid volatile sulfides ( AVS) , total organic carbon ( TOC) , and minerals.
