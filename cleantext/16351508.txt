  The aim of this work was to offer an ecological alternative to conventional observational studies and identify factors potentially associated with cancer incidence in Europe. The incidence of 24 types of cancer in 39 European countries ( 2012) was compared with a long-term mean supply of 68 food items from the FAOSTAT database ( 1993-2011) and some other variables such as smoking , body mass index , raised cholesterol , and socioeconomic indicators.
