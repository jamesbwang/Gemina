  We report the case of a 12-year-old immunocompromised boy with spondylodiscitis of the thoracolumbar spine caused by Aspergillus<pathogen> terreus<pathogen> Microbiologic diagnosis was confirmed by inoculation of aspiration fluid into blood culture bottles. Due to non-compliance , the patient was treated with extended voriconazole therapy ( 23 months) with regular serum drug concentration monitoring and intermittent direct observation therapy in an outpatient clinic.
