  Body dissatisfaction is prevalent among women and associated with subsequent obesity and eating disorders. Exposure to images of bodies of different sizes has been suggested to change the perception of ` normal ' body size in others. We tested whether exposure to different-sized ( otherwise identical) bodies changes perception of own and others ' body size , satisfaction with body size and amount of chocolate consumed.
