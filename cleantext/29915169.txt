  Adamantiades-Behçet disease is a primary systemic vasculitis of unknown origin , that may involve blood vessels of all sizes. The clinical hall-mark of Behçet Disease ( BD) is oral aphtosis , which is often associated with genital aphtae and a variety of skin<symptom> lesions<symptom>. Less common manifestations of BD include thrombophlebitis , central nervous system and ocular inflammation<symptom> which , however , may impact significant morbidity and mortality.
