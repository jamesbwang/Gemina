  The article presents data related to research on the staffing situation of outpatient-and-polyclinic institutions in Western Kazakhstan , conducting medical examinations of the population. The personnel supply and activity of 58 medical institutions of Aktobe , Atyrau , West Kazakhstan regions for 2013-2016 are analyzed. The low availability of outpatient-and-polyclinic institutions by medical personnel of narrow specialties is established. At the same time , there is a tendency to improve the availability of medical personnel for the period under study. The most prosperous situation is in the city of Aktobe.
