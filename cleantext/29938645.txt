  The Danish National Board of Health recommends vitamin D supplement for children under two years , regardless of race or nutrition source. Danish studies have found , that 50-80 % of children of Palestinian and Pakistani origin of the ages 0-16 years had vitamin D deficiency. This case report of vitamin D poisoning<disease> in two immigrant children shows , that there is a group of people , who are not aware of the correct dosage. Doctors as well as healthcare practitioners should be aware of the problem.
