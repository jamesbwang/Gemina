  People are frequently and unintentionally exposed to many chemical compounds , such as environmental pollutants and endocrine-disrupting chemicals ( EDCs) , in food and from the atmosphere. In particular , endocrine-disrupting TBBPA and dioxins are found in human breast milk and in the body. Conventional studies evaluate toxicity by administering a single substance to cells or animals , but evaluation of the toxicity of mixtures of these ingested compounds is essential for `` true '' toxicological assessment.
