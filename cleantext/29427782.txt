  The transcriptome sequencing data of valproic acid ( VPA) model of autism and control rats are presented. VPA model of autism was induced by a single intraperitoneal injection of 600 mg/kg sodium valproic acid to female rats at day 12.5 post-conception , and the control rats were injected with saline.
