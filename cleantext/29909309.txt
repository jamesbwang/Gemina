  Microplastics ( MPs) sized between 0.33 and 5 mm were collected using Manta trawls from ten surface seawater sites in Bohai and Huanghai Seas , China. A total of 1024 ( Bohai Sea) and 132 ( Huanghai Sea) microplastic pieces were classified , including polystyrene foams , polyethylene films and lines , and other plastic pellets , with concentrations of MPs ranging from 3 to 162 particles per 100 m
