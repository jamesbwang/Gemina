  Gas sensors play an undeniable role in most fields of technology in the modern world; they are broadly used for public safety , pollution monitoring , quality control , breath analysis , smart homes and automobiles , and so on. Due to their low cost , high sensitivity , compact size , online detection , ease of use , portability , and low power consumption , metal oxide ( MO) gas sensors have exceptional potential for detection of more than 150 gases.
