  Daily undulating periodization ( DUP) , using daily alterations in repetitions , has been advocated as a superior method of resistance training , while traditional forms of programming for periodization ( Block) have been questioned. Nineteen Division I track and field athletes were assigned to either a 10-week Block or DUP training group. Year and event were controlled. Over the course of the study , there were four testing sessions , which were used to evaluate a variety of strength characteristics , including maximum isometric strength , rate of force development , and one repetition maximum ( 1RM).
