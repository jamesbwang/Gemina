  This study aimed to assess the relationship between weight gain from early adulthood and visceral fat accumulation. The participants were 549 men aged 42 to 64 years who were randomly selected from the local resident registry for the National Institute for Longevity Sciences ' neighbourhood. They were asked to recall their weight at 18 years of age , and then , post-18 weight-change values were calculated for each participant ( their current weight minus their weight at 18).
