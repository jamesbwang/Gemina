  The posthumous organ donation reasoned action scale ( PODRAS) may be the first multidimensional scale measuring individuals ' perceptions , which is developed based on the Theory of Reasoned Action and guided by empirical data. This study moves further to validate the scale and examine its psychometric properties. Two hundred and twenty-one university students participated in the study. Results suggested that the scale has a structure with six dimensions: Mutilation and Imperfectness , Legacy for Family , Altruism , Detachment , Eradication of Hope , and Burden on Family.
