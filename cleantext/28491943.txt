  Urban expansion is one of the major problems in Ethiopia resulting in displacement of the rural people inhabiting areas bordering the cities/towns. It is also resulting in land use land cover ( LULC) changes affecting the livelihoods of the people and the ecosystems ( Messay et al. , 2017; Ganamo , 2013) ( ( 1) , ( 2)). The data presented in this article , therefore shows the spatiotemporal LULC changes of peri-urban expansion areas of Shashemene City.
