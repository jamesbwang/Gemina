  Collagen-peptide supplementation could be an effective remedy to improve hydration , elasticity , and wrinkling in human skin. The aim of this study was to conduct a double-blind , randomized , placebo-controlled trial to clinically evaluate the effect on human skin hydration , wrinkling , and elasticity of Low-molecular-weight Collagen peptide ( LMWCP) with a tripetide ( Gly-X-Y) content > 15 % including 3 % Gly-Pro-Hyp.
