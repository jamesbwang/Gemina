  Purpura is defined as a visible hemorrhage<symptom> in the skin or mucosa , which is not evanescent upon pressure. Proper classification allows a better patient approach due to its multiple diagnoses. Purpuras can be categorized by size , morphology , and other characteristics. The course varies according to the etiology , as do the diagnostic approach and treatment. This review discusses pigmented purpuras and some cutaneous vascular occlusion
