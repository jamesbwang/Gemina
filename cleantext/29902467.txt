  Rett syndrome<disease> ( RTT) is a neurodevelopmental disorder caused by mutations in the MECP2 gene. In the absence of MeCP2/MECP2 , expression of FXYD domain-containing transport regulator 1 ( FXYD1) is deregulated in the frontal cortex ( FC) of mice and humans.
