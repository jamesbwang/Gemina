  In this study , the first survey of anuran species in the Cancão Municipal Natural Park is presented , a protected area of approximately 370 hectares of Amazonian forest<disease> located in the northwest center region of the state of Amapá , Brazil. The work was performed during the dry and rainy season , through active visual and auditory survey , totaling 216 man hours of sampling effort.
