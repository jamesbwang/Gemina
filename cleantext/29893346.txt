  Myeloid sarcoma<disease> can be occurred bofore acute myeloid leukemia<disease> , concomitantly , at relapse or after allogenic stem cell transplantation , rarely. In this investigation , we reported a 38-year-old woman with a history of AML relapsed 9 months after allo-SCT as an MS in the bilateral breast. She underwent bilateral mastectomy. The patient was a candidate for systemic chemotherapy. An intermediate dose of cytarabine with mitoxantrone was considered for the patient , and it was repeated at the specified intervals. Currently , the patient is still being followed up , and there is no sign of local or systemic recurrence.
