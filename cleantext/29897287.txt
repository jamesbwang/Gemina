  Urinary tract obstruction represents a common cause of kidney injury across the human lifespan , resulting in chronic kidney disease and end stage renal disease. Yet the extent of obstructive renal damage can be heterogeneous between individuals , implying the existence of unknown mechanisms that protect against or accelerate kidney injury.
