  Simultaneous solar energy conversion and storage is receiving increasing interest for better utilization of the abundant yet intermittently available sunlight. Photoelectrodes driving nonspontaneous reversible redox reactions in solar-powered redox cells ( SPRCs) , which can deliver energy via the corresponding reverse reactions , present a cost-effective and promising approach for direct solar energy harvesting and storage.
