  Chronic arsenic ( As) unprotection in drinking water can lead to Type 2 Diabetes Mellitus ( T2DM). The purpose of this study was to investigate the association between chronic As in drinking water and the prevalence of T2DM. A study was conducted in targeted urban areas of Peshawar city of KPK , Pakistan , where drinking water is heavily contaminated with chronic arsenic. Participants protected to arsenic were selected from Kohat city of KPK , Pakistan ( where people consumed water that is free from As contamination) and treated as the control group.
