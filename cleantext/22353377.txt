  Recent several studies indicated that a more restrictive dietary intake guideline for cadmium should be made for sufficient health protection. In the present study , we showed the references level of food cadmium intake ( FCd) and total cadmium intake ( TCd) for renal dysfunction by using benchmark dose ( BMD) approach. 342 subjects living in a control and a cadmium polluted area were included in this study.
