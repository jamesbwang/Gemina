  While existing research documents the impact of parental rejection on sexual minorities , the present study extends this research to include a subtler , yet potentially more pervasive , challenge facing sexual minorities and their parents , with lasting implications for mental health. Parental unfinished business refers to persistent , unresolved negative thoughts and feelings toward one 's parents and is investigated here as a result of parental rejection of their sexual minority sons ' sexual orientation.
