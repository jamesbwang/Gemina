  There is a little consensus regarding the association of pemphigus with autoimmune thyroid diseases. While this association had been confirmed by some observational studies , others had refuted it. We aimed to study the association between pemphigus and Hashimoto 's thyroiditis , Grave 's disease , and thyroid cancer using a large-scale real-life computerized database.
