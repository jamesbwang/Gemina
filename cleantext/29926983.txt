  The C We investigated 11,536 RhD + Brazilian blood donors. All samples were phenotyped for D , C , c , E , e , and C C This study showed the prevalence of the RhD + phenotype in the Brazilian population , and that through the molecular study , it was possible to differentiate the RHCE * Ce .08.01 and RHCE * Ce .09.01 alleles. The phenotype frequency was similar from Black people ( 1 %) and different from Caucasians , Finns , and Latvians.
