  Drug addiction is a disorder in which drug seeking persists despite aversive consequences. While it is well documented in animal models of drug sensitization that repeated drug exposure enhances positive incentive motivation for drug and natural reinforcers , its effect on negative incentive motivation , defined here as the motivation to avoid a cued aversive outcome , remains an open question.
