  The purpose of this study was to investigate the determinants of the health-related quality of life of elderly people in Korea by applying the Andersen model. This study was a descriptive survey. Participants were 240 elderly people over 65 years of age living in a community. A hierarchical multiple regression was performed to analyze the factors related to the health-related quality of life , including predisposing , enabling , and need factors , and health behaviors. Need factors , that is , self-reported health status , were the most significant in influencing the health-related quality of life of the elderly people; predisposing factors were age and education level , and enabling factors were income and activities of daily living. The findings of this study can be used to develop a community-based intervention program and to establish a policy for improving the quality of life of elderly people.
