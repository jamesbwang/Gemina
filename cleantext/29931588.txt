  This study aims to investigate the effects of low-level laser therapy ( LLLT) combined to a functional exercise program on treatment of FM. A double-blind and placebo-controlled randomized clinical trial composed of 22 women divided into two groups: placebo group ( functional exercise program associated with placebo phototherapy n = 11) and laser group ( same exercise program associated with active phototherapy; n = 11).
