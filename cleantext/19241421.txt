  Leptomeningeal metastases from the prostate are extremely rare , with few cases described in the medical literature ( antemortem prevalence of less than 0.03 %). Prostatic leptomeningeal metastases harbour a poor prognosis with a median survival after diagnosis of 15.7 weeks.
