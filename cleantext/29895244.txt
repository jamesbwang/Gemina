  A variety of fungi<pathogen> , plants , and their different tissues are used in Traditional Chinese Medicine to improve health , and some of them are recommended for dietary therapy. Many of these plants and fungi<pathogen> contain antifungal proteins and peptides which suppress spore germination and hyphal growth in phytopathogenic fungi<pathogen> The aim of this article is to review antifungal proteins produced by medicinal plants and fungi<pathogen> used in Chinese medicine which also possess anticancer and human immunodeficiency virus-1 ( HIV-1) enzyme inhibitory activities.
