  Natural sciences are still considered as typical male fields , while humanities are interpreted as typical female topics. Economic , social but also biological factors are discussed to influence the choice of study field. In the present study , the impact of prenatal sex hormone exposure- estimated by 2D: 4D ratio- on the choice of study field was analyzed. Two hundred Viennese students between the ages 18 and 28 years were enrolled. Lengths of the index finger and the ring finger were measured directly from the hand of the participants. 2D: 4D ratios were calculated.
