  In this data article , analysis of surface water vapour density in Southeast , Nigeria were reported. The meteorological data were obtained for the period of 39 years between 1973 and 2012 from National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration ( NOAA) Climatology Centre. Five stations considered in the research area includes: Enugu , Onitsha , Abakaliki , Aba and Ihiala. Descriptive statistics were used to show an increase in monthly variation of surface water vapour density ( SWVD) minimum value of about 7.15 g/m
