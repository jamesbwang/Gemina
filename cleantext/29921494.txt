  Olfactory neuroblastoma ( ONB) is a kind of rare and complex head and neck tumor. The reports on this field are very scarce due to the low morbidity. Here , we summarized the clinical features and prognosis of ONB through analysis of 10 cases , and determined the phosphorylation status of some molecules known to be involved in carcinogesis such as Akt , Erk , Stat3 and Stat5 in ONB tissue.
