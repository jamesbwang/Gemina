 Dermatoscopy is a noninvasive , painless , and easily repeatable technique that reveals interesting and characteristic features associated with various cutaneous conditions. Lichen nitidus is a rare dermatosis of childhood. We describe the dermatoscopic features in 8 cases of lichen nitidus , which can aid noninvasive diagnosis. The report serves to emphasize the need for examining these lesions<symptom> using both polarized and nonpolarized modes.
