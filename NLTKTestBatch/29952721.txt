 Chronic arsenic ( As) exposure in drinking water can lead to the Type 2 Diabetes Mellitus ( T2DM). The purpose of this study was to investigate the association between chronic As in drinking water and prevalence of T2DM. A cross sectional study was conducted in urban areas of Peshawar city of KPK , Pakistan where drinking water is heavily contaminated with chronic arsenic. The participant 's unexposed to arsenic were recruited as control group from Kohat city of KPK , Pakistan , where people consumed free As-contaminated water.
