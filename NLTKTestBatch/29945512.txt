 The World Society for Pediatric and Congenital Heart Surgery ( WSPCHS) is the largest professional organization in the world dedicated to pediatric and congenital heart surgery. The purpose of this article is to document the first decade of the history of WSPCHS from its formation in 2006 , to summarize the current status<symptom> of WSPCHS , and to consider the future of WSPCHS.
