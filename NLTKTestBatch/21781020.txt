 Academic burnout is prevalent among university students , although understanding of what predicts burnout is limited. This study aimed to test the direct and indirect relationship between two dimensions of perfectionism ( Perfectionistic Concerns and Perfectionistic Strivings) and the three elements of Academic Burnout ( Exhaustion<symptom> , Inadequacy , and Cynicism) through Repetitive Negative Thinking.
