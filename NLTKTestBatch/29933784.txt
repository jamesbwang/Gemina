 This article summarizes current knowledge regarding the incidence of and risk factors associated with recurrent lower gastrointestinal hemorrhage<symptom>. The literature regarding medical , endoscopic , and surgical methods to prevent rebleeding from diverticulosis , angioectasia , and chronic hemorrhagic radiation proctopathy is reviewed. In addition , the evidence for endoscopic clipping as primary prophylaxis against postpolypectomy bleeding is explored.
