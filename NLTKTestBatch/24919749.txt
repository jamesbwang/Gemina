 Native American youth aged 10 to 19 years are disproportionately affected by type 2 diabetes. Intergenerational programs may improve health in tribal communities. We evaluated Together on Diabetes , a diabetes prevention and management program , among 257 participating Native American youths with or at risk for type 2 diabetes and their adult caregivers. Feasibility , acceptability , and demographic data were collected from 226 adult caregivers. Results indicated that engaging adult caregivers was feasible , acceptable , and effective.
