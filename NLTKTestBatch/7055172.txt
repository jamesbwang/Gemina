 The aim of this paper is to evaluate the efficacy and safety of three different norepinephrine dosing regimens for preventing spinal hypotension<symptom> in cesarean section. In this randomized double-blinded controlled study , 120 parturients scheduled for elective section delivery under spinal anesthesia were assigned to 1 of 4 groups.
