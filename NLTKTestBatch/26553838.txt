 This article explores the possibilities of applying ear acupuncture to newborn infants. In addition to the use of needle acupuncture , there are also noninvasive applications that are preferred for patients in this particular age group. Based on the limited literature , ear acupuncture could be an effective nonpharmacologic approach for the treatment of pain in newborn infants and for addressing symptoms<symptom> of neonatal abstinence syndrome<symptom>. However , studies on safety , side-effects , and other indications are still lacking.
