  Cloacal swab samples collected from 538 migratory waterfowl along the Mississippi Migratory Bird flyway in 2013 were tested for porcine epidemic diarrhea<disease> virus and porcine deltacoronavirus. Neither virus<pathogen> was detected in any of the samples , indicating that waterfowl likely did not contribute to the rapid spread of these viruses<pathogen> within central US.
