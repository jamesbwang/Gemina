  Multiple reassortant strains of novel , highly pathogenic avian influenza<disease> A have recently emerged and spread over the world. Here we report on a 68-year-old woman in Jiangsu , China , with influenza<disease> A<pathogen> ( H7N4<pathogen>) infection and associated illness , which strongly demonstrating the ability of the virus to spread from animals to humans and thus emphasizing the importance of continuous surveillance of the emerging viruses.
