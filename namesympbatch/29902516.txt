  Recently , Veillonella<pathogen> infantium was isolated from tongue biofilm of a Thai child and established as a novel Veillonella<pathogen> species. In this study , a species-specific primer was designed to identify V. infantium on the basis of the sequence of the 70 kDa heat shock<symptom> protein ( dnaK) gene of Veillonella<pathogen> infantium JCM 31738
